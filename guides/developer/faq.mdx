## Stablecoin Conversions

<details>
<summary>
<h3 id="get-started">How can I get started with conversions?</h3>
</summary>
<div>

The [stablecoin conversion guide] takes you through the simple process. Reach out to [Support] if you need assistance.

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>
<h3 id="fees">What are the conversion fees?</h3>
</summary>
<div>

None. Conversions between the [supported asset pairs][conversion-assets] incure no fees.

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>
<h3 id="limits">Are there any conversion limits?</h3>
</summary>
<div>

By default, there is a $500,000 USD transaction limit per conversion.
Contact [Support](https://support.paxos.com) for additional details.

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>
<h3 id="dust">What is dust?</h3>
</summary>
<div>

Dust is a small amount of an asset leftover from a transaction.
Dust can accumulate when converting [assets with different precisions][conversion-assets].

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>
<h3 id="cancel">Can I cancel a conversion?</h3>
</summary>
<div>

Most conversions execute near instantaneously; however, under rare conditions a settlement delay may occur.
You can use the [Cancel Stablecoin Conversion][cancel] endpoint to cancel a pending conversion.

</div>
</details>
