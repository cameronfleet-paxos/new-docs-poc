<details>
<summary>
<h3 id="assets">Do I need to have the right amount of assets before creating the transaction?</h3>
</summary>
<div>

No.
You can create and approve the transaction first and then deposit [assets] when ready.
Settlement only takes place when the transaction is [affirmed][status] and all the required assets have been deposited.

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>
<h3 id="notifications">Why am I not receiving email notifications when testing in Sandbox?</h3>
</summary>
<div>

Notifications are off by default in Sandbox. Contact [Support][support] to turn them on.

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>
<h3 id="testing">Which assets are supported for Sandbox testing?</h3>
</summary>
<div>

You can fund your Sandbox Account with the supported [assets] or those listed in [Create Sandbox Deposit][deposit].
For most setups, use the assets you can trade, transfer or withdraw for testing.

</div>
</details>
