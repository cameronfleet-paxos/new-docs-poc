<Tip>

- Use [Get Fiat Deposit Instructions][get-fiat-deposit-instructions] to check the status of the instructions before initiating a deposit.
- Before initiating a withdrawal, use [Get Fiat Account][get-fiat-account] to ensure the account status is `APPROVED`.
- When transferring funds, use [List Transfers][list-transfers] and [Get Profile Balance][get-profile-balance] to check on the transfer status and Profile balance changes.

</Tip>
